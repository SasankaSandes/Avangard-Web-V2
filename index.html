Our Strategy

Avangard Capital’s quantitative investment strategy pairs human expertise with machine intelligence to create actively managed, low-volatility portfolios for wholesale investors. We focus on uncorrelated assets and disciplined risk control to deliver robust, long-term performance. Every portfolio is carefully built for resilience, stability, and reliable growth so your capital is protected and positioned to thrive in any market environment.


ALFRED’s role is to generate an efficient portfolio of 20-50 stocks/ETFs, which are statistically diverse (uncorrelated). This portfolio aim to have the following characteristics:

  • Strong medium to long-term returns
  • Low Volatility (i.e. Sharpe ratio > 2.5, K-Ratio > 2)
  • Statistically diverse (reducing risk)
  • Minimal Trading (hedging when appropriate)
  • Long-term investment (reducing tax liabilities)
  • Investing in Large-cap” stocks (generally > $5B)
