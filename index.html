Our Strategy

Avangard’s strategy pairs human expertise with machine intelligence to create actively managed, low-volatility portfolios. We focus on uncorrelated assets and disciplined risk control to deliver robust, long-term performance. Every portfolio is carefully built for resilience, stability, and reliable growth so your capital is protected and positioned to thrive in any market environment.


ALFRED’s role is to generate an efficient portfolio of 20-50 stocks/ETFs, which are statistically diverse (uncorrelated). This portfolio aim to have the following characteristics:

  • Strong medium to long-term returns
  • Low Volatility (i.e. Sharpe ratio > 2.5, K-Ratio > 2)
  • Statistically diverse (reducing risk)
  • Minimal Trading (hedging when appropriate)
  • Long-term investment (reducing tax liabilities)
  • Investing in Large-cap” stocks (generally > $5B)

A.L.F.R.E.D.

Adaptive Learning For Ranking Equities & Derivatives

Meet A.L.F.R.E.D, our Co-Manager in the Markets:  A.L.F.R.E.D. isn’t just an algorithm. It’s an adaptive intelligence partner that supports our investment team by continuously analysing market behaviour, identifying inefficiencies, and balancing opportunity with risk.Together, human experience and machine precision deliver an edge that’s both disciplined and dynamic.

A.L.F.R.E.D. Adaptive Learning For Ranking Equities & Derivatives is a proprietary platform created by our founder, Alfred Eggo. This advanced technology analyses thousands of financial variables, integrating behavioural finance, risk-adjusted momentum models, and machine learning to adapt to shifting markets and uncover patterns that traditional approaches often overlook.​​

Every portfolio and investment decision is shaped by the precision of A.L.F.R.E.D. and the operational stewardship of experienced professionals, so investors benefit from innovative technology and seamless execution.​​
